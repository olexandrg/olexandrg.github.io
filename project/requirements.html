Final Project Requirements

Project Goal
The Portland Historical Tours© website will offer a 100% online solution to marketing and customer reservation needs. The site will list the 3 major tours the company offers, as well as their costs and descriptions. It will attract new clients with the use of social media and news updates marketing, and offer the capability for the user to book their tours through the website.
Key Features
Tour Descriptions | Online Reservations | Social Media | News Updates
Indended Audience
Visitors, tourists, anyone seeking to expand their knowledge of "old Portland", or simply locals trying to find something to do on a reasonable budget.
Main Issues
Lack of marketing – the business receives new customers based on word of mouth and phone book.

Multiple competitors are ahead on this with social media pages, online descriptions and photos. The site should rightfully advertise that the company has been in business for over 30 years – longer than any competitor.

Unclear or undefined purpose – what makes this company stand out among the competition?
Goal for Visitors
The users should be able to call the company, fill out a contact form, search information about company tours, and be able to book a tour.
